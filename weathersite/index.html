SITE PLANNING

Name Ideas: Clear Sky Weather

Motto Ideas: Clearing the confusion of the weather and weather safety

Domain Name: clearskyweather.com

Icon:

SITE PURPOSE

Clear Sky Weather's purpose as a weather site is to provide information such as the weather, road conditions, if it's good weather for outdoor activities, safety sections. The safety sections will have a section on sun protection, running & biking safety, and water saftey as idaho has a very high drowning rate among children.

SAFETY

For water safety it will have statistics, preventative measures, and tips for water safety for all ages and links to places that teach water safety. For running and biking safety it will have things to be aware of in certain weather conditions, equiptment to wear, and anything else pertaining to it. For the sun protection it will go over times of day to go outside where uv isn't so harsh, tanning, and sun screen.

WEATHER

The website will also provide the weather for the areas in idaho mentioned on the project. It'll cover when the sun sets and rises, wind, chance of rain, and so on.

OUTDOOR ACTIVITIES

The website will show a page of suitable recreational activities for the weather that day, and for the week. It'll provide places to do the activities listed and link to show additional resources.

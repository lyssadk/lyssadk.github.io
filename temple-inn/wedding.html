We have a reception hall that can seat up to 200 people. It includes tables, menus, chairs, and a serving area. We ask that you let us know how many people will be attending 2 weeks in advance.

Included in our reception area is a long head table as an option for the bride and groom to sit with a few others facing the rest of the guests.

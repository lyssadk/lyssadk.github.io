WATER SAFETY IN IDAHO

Idaho has the second highest percentage of accidental drownings due to all the canals and rivers. Because of the lack of pools in most areas, parents have a hard time teaching their children how to swim or learn to be safe in the water. What most people don't know is even babies as young as 6 months can learn how to float in the water by themselves. Mountain Wave swimming ... offers 20 min private lessons to teach children how to be safe in the water.
